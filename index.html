Warrior Builds
Warriors

Warriors in the Dragon Age The Veilguard serve as frontline fighters, specializing in heavy Armor and melee Weapons. Their primary functions include absorbing damage and controlling enemy positions during combat. They are equipped with heavy armor and typically wield swords, axes, and shields. As tanks, Warriors draw enemy attention and protect more vulnerable allies. Their key attributes are high durability and physical strength, making them formidable in battle. Veilguard Warriors provide a solid defensive foundation, allowing other classes to focus on offense or support.

Rogue Builds
Rogues

Rogues are incredibly quick and agile, capable of dodging enemy attacks and repositioning themselves in combat to avoid damage. They can move swiftly across the battlefield, making them difficult targets to hit, and ideal for hit-and-run tactics. Rogues can specialize in either dual-wielding weapons like daggers or using a bow for ranged attacks. Dual-wielding Rogues focus on close-quarters combat, delivering rapid, lethal strikes. Ranged Rogues excel at staying in the backlines, using bows to deal consistent damage from a distance.

Mage Builds
Mages

Mages primary functions include controlling crowds, dealing area damage, and supporting the party with healing and buffs. They are equipped with magical Staves, Dagger and Orb, and light Armor to maintain mobility while casting powerful spells from a distance. As disruptors, Mages control the battlefield by incapacitating Enemies and weakening large groups with area-of-effect spells.
