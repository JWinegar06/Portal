Death Caller Builds
Death Caller

The Death Callers are mages who embrace the dark. They harness their knowledge from the Necropolis and excel in inflicting necrotic test Necrotic Damage to drain the life force of enemies to sustain themselves

Envoker Builds
Evoker

The Evoker specialization excels in crowd control by applying Cold Damage to freeze enemies in their tracks and and delivers powerful magical attacks to take down enemies.

Spellblade Builds
Spellblade

The Spellblade specialization is a combination of close-quarters combat and Electricity magic to engage their enemies in combat. They infuse their attacks with lightning which applies Shocked, and or Sundered effects
