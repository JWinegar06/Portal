Duelist Builds
Duelist

The Duelist specialization utilizes parries and dodges to take advantage of their enemies' missed attacks. They can strike enemies quickly with their advanced agility and their unique skills can inflict Necrotic Damage in a large AoE.

Saboteur Builds
Saboteur

The Saboteur specializes in using explosive tools, turrets, and contraptions to weaken their enemies and take control of the fight. Their unique abilities inflict strong Physical Damage on their enemies.

Veil Ranger Builds
Veil Ranger

The Veil Ranger excels in ranged combat by imbuing their arrows with powerful magic to defeat groups of enemies. Their unique abilities can inflict Electricity effects.
