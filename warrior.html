Champion Builds

Champion

The Champion specialization uses a Sword and Shield as its primary choice of weapon. They utilize their shield both as a weapon and a defensive tool, by incorporating offensive moves against enemies while also protecting their allies behind their backs. Their unique abilities use Fire Damage against their enemies.

Reaper Builds

Reaper

The Reaper specialization can switch between Two-Handed weapons and Sword and Shield equipment. Their unique abilities can inflict Necrotic Damage that slowly drains the lives of their enemies to sustain their own.

Slayer Builds

Slayer

The Slayer specialization focuses on Two-Handed Weapon abilities. They charge head-first into battle with the goal of breaking down the enemies' defenses. Their unique abilities inflict strong physical testPhysical Damage on their enemies.
